YouTube Comment Analyzer




To generate a statistics report, paste the URL for the website in the textbox above, then enter the number of pages you want (100 comments per page) , then if your comments are not in English, you can select the box with the "Translate, if text is not English" and finally press the "Generate Stats" button.

As of now, this application can only function with YouTube but changes are being made to make it compatible with other websites.

Instructions: How to Generate Stats with Youtube URL

Step 1: Copy and paste the Youtube URL into the box. Step 2: Click the 'Generate Stats' button. Step 3: View the generated dashboard with your stats!
